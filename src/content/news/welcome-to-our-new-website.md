We're pleased to launch our new Scoonie Medical Practice website. The site has been completely rebuilt with you in mind — making it quicker and easier to:

- **Book an appointment** — find out how to contact us and what to expect
- **Order repeat prescriptions** — step-by-step guides for every ordering method
- **Register as a new patient** — a clear walkthrough with downloadable forms
- **Find self-referral services** — discover NHS services you can access directly, without a GP appointment
- **Get urgent help** — know who to call when the surgery is closed

We've also added a care navigator tool on the homepage to help point you in the right direction if you're not sure where to start.

If you have any feedback on the new site, we'd love to hear from you. You can [get in touch](/contact/get-in-touch/) or [leave feedback](/contact/feedback/).
