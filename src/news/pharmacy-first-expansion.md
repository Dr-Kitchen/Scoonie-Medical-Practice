The Pharmacy First Scotland service has been extended to cover additional conditions. This means your local pharmacist can now assess and treat a wider range of common health problems — free on the NHS, without a GP appointment.

## What this means for you

If you have one of the conditions covered by Pharmacy First, visiting your pharmacy may be faster than calling us. Your pharmacist can prescribe medication on the spot if needed.

## Conditions covered

See our [Pharmacy First page](/get-help/pharmacy-first/) for the full list of conditions.

## Why this matters

Pharmacy First helps more people get treated quickly, while freeing up GP appointments for those who need them most. We encourage all patients to consider their pharmacy as a first port of call for minor ailments.
