Annual flu vaccinations are now available. The flu vaccine is free on the NHS for people who are at higher risk from flu.

## Who is eligible?

You may be eligible for a free NHS flu vaccination if you are:

- Aged 65 or over
- Pregnant
- A child aged 2–17 (nasal spray vaccine)
- Living with a long-term health condition (such as diabetes, asthma, or heart disease)
- A carer for someone with a health condition
- Severely obese (BMI over 40)

## How to book

Call us on [{{ site.phone }}](tel:{{ site.phone | replace(' ', '') }}) or book through Patient Access. You can also get a flu jab at many local pharmacies — no appointment needed.

## Why bother?

Flu can be serious, particularly for vulnerable people. Getting vaccinated protects you and those around you.
