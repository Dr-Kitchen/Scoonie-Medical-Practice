We're pleased to announce the launch of our new website. We've redesigned it from scratch with one goal in mind: making it as easy as possible for patients to find what they need.

## What's new

- Clearer navigation — find appointments, prescriptions, and services in one or two clicks
- Better mobile experience — the site works on any device
- Improved accessibility — we've built the site to meet WCAG 2.1 AA accessibility standards
- Useful information about Pharmacy First and self-referral services

## We'd love your feedback

If you have any comments about the new website — good or bad — please use our [feedback form](/contact/feedback/) to let us know. Your feedback helps us improve.
